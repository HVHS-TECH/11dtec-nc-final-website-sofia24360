About Our School

Alfea College for Faries located in Magix, is one of the most pretigous institutions for faries. At Alfea College for Fairies, students learn how to control their power, fight evil, learn new spells and be guardians of their realms. Alfea offers a variety of advanced courses like Potionology, metamorphosimbiosis, magiphilosophy and good manners. All these are taught by our skilled faculty. The students of Alfea will live wihtin the college itself. Alfea is also equiped with a simulation room, greenhouse and amphitheater to further advance our students educations. Under the guidance of our Headmistress Faragonda students will strive in Alfea, obtain their Enchantix transformation, and graduate.
