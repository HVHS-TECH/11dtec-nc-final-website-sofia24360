Alfea College for Fairies is one of the most prestigious all female boarding school for young fairies in Magix. We aim to prepare young fairies for their future as guardian fairies protecting their realms and worlds.

In Alfea College for Fairies, students work towards becoming the guardian fairies of their planet. They learn how to control their power, fight the evil, and learn new spells.
